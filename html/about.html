ABOUT US

KTG is a Narrow woven fabric manufacturing company where we make products for Consumers and various Narrow woven fabric products for OEM Companies.

Our only objective is to provide consumer products that are of high quality hence enhancing people`s lives.

For this we strive to develop and create products that we believe can make a difference in the lives of the consumers.

Our employees work hard to connect with our Partners & Suppliers to help KTG become a successful and enterprising company. We firmly believe that the growth of the company and the individuals working for it is inseparable.
